Welcome to glowing 5!

Here's how to play:

  • Answer multiple-choice questions correctly to score points.
  • You need to answer 5 questions correctly to win.
  • If you answer 3 questions incorrectly, or run out of time, you lose.
  • Out of 4 options only 1 is correct, you can skip questions.
  • Feel free to restart from the beginning at any time.
  • Check your score on the leaderboard.

Good luck! Share with friends and have fun!
